This morning, when I entered my office I was greeted by hundreds of Nexus 4 cases. Yesterday [our team](https://www.sensible.dtu.dk/?page_id=1096 "great team") of hardworking,  thoughtful & highly intelligent grad students were wasting their years of education, doing sweat-shop level work at an undisclosed location in the greater Copenhagen area. Their task: rooting & flashing more than one thousand Nexus 4 phones for our SensibleDTU project. Due to creative hacking, the amount of time spent was about 9 hours.
Being a (relatively) old man with little/no staying power, I left a bit early to let the rest of the team finish up.  Little did I know that they saved about 1/3 of the nexus outer boxes, hauled them back to DTU and used them to decorate my office.  The result is shown below :)
