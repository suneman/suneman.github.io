This week I'm visiting the [Max Planck Institute for the Physics of Complex Systems](http://www.mpipks-dresden.mpg.de/ "mpi pks") in Dresden, participating in the workshop *[Mathematical Physics of Complex Networks: From Graph Theory to Biological Physics](http://www.pks.mpg.de/~mapcon12/).*

[![](/assets/20120515_mapcon12/dresden.jpeg "dresden")](http://sunelehmann.com/2012/05/15/mapcon12/dresden/)

It's quite the honor to be invited to speak at a conference full of [real physicists & bona fide graph theorists](http://www.pks.mpg.de/~mapcon12/Teilnehmerliste_Web_100512.pdf) (although it's putting my softening brain hard at work: there seems to be a lot more analytical results here than I'm used to these days).
