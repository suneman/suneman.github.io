Slowly but surely [NetSci 2013](http://netsci2013.net/) is coming to life! We're now ready with the *official call for satellite workshops.* If you have an idea, don't hesitate to send it to Philipp - we're in the process of securing a great venue for the workshops, close to the NetSci school. Come to think of it, I think I might try to set up a workshop of my own! Keep an eye on the blog for more details.
