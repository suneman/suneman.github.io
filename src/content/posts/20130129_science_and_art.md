Earlier this year, I was lucky enough to be awarded a Villum Foundation Young Investigator grant. A couple of days ago the Foundation's Annual Report came out [[dk link](http://veluxfondene.dk/C12576AB0041A865/0/F5D57E47E87E7D00C1257AF7001FF438/$file/VeluxAarsskrift2012-DK-WEB2.pdf), [en link](http://veluxfoundations.dk/C12576AB00426565/0/56E50ED58E1A9081C1257AF70021B07E/$file/VeluxAarsskrift2012-UK-WEB2.pdf). note: this is a 10mb download], with a very nice cover that features graphics designed by yours truly.
[![villum_front_page](/assets/20130129_science_and_art/villum_front_page_0048705f.jpg)](/assets/20130129_science_and_art/villum_front_page.jpg)
