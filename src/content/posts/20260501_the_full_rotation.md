I have to admit that I've had a ton of fun redesigning my site in collaboration AI. This blog is still written exclusively by me, but pretty much everything else has been generated by AI. 

Many of the details are stil my own silly ideas. A key design element are the portraits in the header. I had the idea to generate silly images of myself, but they were all generated by the new ChatGPT imagegen2. But there IS a generative art element. I started from an existing portrait and had claude generate a list of colors + iconic costumes. I had help from ChatGPT with the prompt, then used Claude to generate 20 portaits and handpicked the 15 best one. The site chooses a random one is picked on each page load. 

I don't think anyone wants to load the page enough times to see them all, but they're fun, so I've collected them all here in this post.
