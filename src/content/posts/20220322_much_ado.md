My hallmate from Valkendorf's Kollegium 2001-2004 Line Friis Frederiksen, was the MC on the day. Small world.

Official photos from the day.

The true highlight of the day, however, was when I had to go on stage. My last name is first alphabetically, so I was the first person on stage. Prior to the ceremony we had been carefully briefed about where to stand, members of the Danish Royal Family weren't shaking hands yet and much more. But what I hadn't anticipated is that the minister and the crown princess *had not* received that briefing. So when I got on stage and walked to my designated spot (where the Crown Princess gave me a warm handshake), there was a moment of confusion.

This confusion was picked up by *[Billedbladet](https://www.billedbladet.dk)*, a Danish gossip magazine which subsequently ran a story entitled (my translation): ***[Confusion on stage: Crown Princess Mary helped get things under control](https://www.billedbladet.dk/kongelige/danmark/forvirring-oppe-paa-scenen-kronprinsesse-mary-hjalp-med-faa-styr-paa-tingene)***. If you click through, there's even a video of the "incident".
