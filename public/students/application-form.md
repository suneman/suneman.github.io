# Project application form

For prospective Master's or Bachelor's students in the Social Complexity Lab at DTU.

## Instructions

Please answer all questions below. Use as much or as little detail as you find appropriate. Return the file as plain text (`.md` or `.txt`) and email it to <sljo@dtu.dk>.

## Questions

**Q1.** What is your study line?

**Q2.** List your programming experience.

**Q3.** List your math courses and skills.

**Q4.** List any other skills you consider relevant.

**Q5.** What is the best class you have taken at DTU, and why?

**Q6.** What is the worst class you have taken at DTU, and why?

**Q7.** What grade do you aim to receive for your project?
