Sune Lehmann

Professor of Networks and Complexity Science, DTU Compute
Professor of Social Data Science, University of Copenhagen

I study quantitative patterns in social systems using large-scale data. My work draws on physics, machine learning, and statistics to understand complex networks, human mobility, sleep, disease transmission, information spreading, and social media dynamics.

With Laura Alessandretti, I direct the Social Complexity Lab.
